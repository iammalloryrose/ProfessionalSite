Project Overview

Conducted laboratory analysis of soil samples including pH testing, EC measurement, and particle size separation. Developed visual maps of soil properties to analyze environmental impacts.

Tools & Techniques

  • Laboratory instrumentation (pH meter, EC probe)
  • QGIS for spatial mapping
  • Image analysis for soil texture classification

Outcomes

Revealed correlations between soil texture and moisture retention zones, supporting recommendations for sustainable soil use practices.
