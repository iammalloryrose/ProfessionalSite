Project Overview

This project analyzed subsurface soil conductivity data from a Texas pecan orchard. I processed over 40,000 electrical conductivity readings to assess root zone health and identify spatial patterns.

Tools & Techniques

  • Surfer for heatmap visualization
  • QGIS for mapping & spatial overlays
  • Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) for subsurface structure
  • Fieldwork: soil coring, EC/pH sampling

Outcomes

Created high-resolution maps to inform irrigation and tree health strategies. This data supported long-term orchard sustainability efforts.
